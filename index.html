About Us

Where Artistry Meets Elegance

Founded in 2015, Yumi Nail Salon has become a sanctuary for those who appreciate the finer details of nail care. Our mission is simple — to deliver exceptional service, creative designs, and an experience that leaves you feeling pampered and beautiful.

Every visit is crafted with care, from the moment you step into our elegant space to the final brushstroke of your perfect manicure.

Certified Experts

Our team holds international certifications in nail artistry and care.

Premium Products

We use only the finest, salon-grade, and non-toxic products.

Personalized Care

Every service is tailored to your unique style and preferences.
